About

VHP is a B2B virtual influencer company. We develop and manage virtual humans that provide marketing services to companies. Our mission is to revolutionise influencer marketing through powerful storytelling and cultural diversity.

Services

VHP offers two branches of services: The All-Stars and The Powerhouse.


The All-Stars: a team of virtual influencers produced and owned by VHP, which is ready to help brands build genuine connections with consumers.


The Powerhouse: production of Virtual Avatars, to represent real human beings, and Bespoke Influencers to represent brands and companies.
